LAMDA
LAMDA
LAMDA is a 2-channel 100 kHz 12-bit data sampling system, built with an STM32F405 ARM microcontroller board. LAMDA captures multiple seconds of data at a time and saves it to an SD card, synchronizing samples against a pulse-per-second signal from a GPS unit.
Toolkits used:
  • STM32Cube development system and hardware abstraction layer

mac-plus-serkey
