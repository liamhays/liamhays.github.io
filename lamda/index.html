LAMDA

LAMDA is a portable data acquisition unit that captures 2 analog channels at 12 bits at about 111,000 samples per second per channel. It uses an Adafruit Feather STM32F405 Express and a microSD card. LAMDA also synchronizes the recorded data to a pulse-per-second signal generated by a GPS unit.
